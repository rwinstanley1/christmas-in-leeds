Festive Food and Drink

Heres our selection of the best festive treats leeds has to offer. From food festivals to a pint in a victorian pub, you're never short of choice.

Food Festivals

Eat North:

The Eat North Christmas Special is bringing the popular food festival back for a special winter edition. This food festival promises a range of festive (and not so festive treats) from some of the most popular leeds street food stalls. Saturday 8th December at North Brewing Co.

Belgrave Christmas Feast

The perfect one stop Christmas stop. In the main bar is a range of festive food trucks, with the bar serving mulled wine and cider as well as their standard range of drinks. Upstairs in the gig room is a special Christmas themed arts fair with loads of local, handmade gifts available. The Belgrave Christmas Feast will be on 14th December

Roasts

The Adelphi

The Victorian feel of this pub makes it the perfect festive destination for a Roast dinner. With wood panelling and a roaring fire, and importantly reasonable pricing, this pub always has a great atmosphere. 3-5 Hunslet Road,LS10 1JQ

Kirkstall Bridge Inn

If you’re looking for a destination away from the shopping crowds of the city centre, Kirkstall Bridge pub is the place to go. With excellent beers brewed by Kirkstall brewery, great roasts and great views of the canal, this is a lovely destination for a cold December afternoon. 12 Bridge Road, LS5 3BW

Drinks

Thor's Tipi

A huge festive themed Tipi outside of Leeds art gallery. With furs, a fire pit, mulled wine and hot cider, it is the perfect festive drink spot in the city centre. In Victoria Gardens, 16th November to 31st December.

Whitelocks

Whitelocks is (allegedly) the oldest pub in the city centre and it definitely feels like that inside. With a bar that’s not been changed since the late 1800s, it is a perfect stop of when you are doing your Christmas shopping as it is right next to the Trinity. With a range of classic beers including Timothy Taylor’s Landlord, and the sister bar the Turks Head next door having a great range of craft beer, there is something for everyone. Turk's Head Yard, LS1 6HB
