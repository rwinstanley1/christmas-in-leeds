Festive Markets

In the Christmas period it's a necessity to experience the magic of a Christmas market or two! Here's our recommendation of the best festive markets in Leeds

Christkindelmarkt

In its 18th year, Leeds Christkindelmarkt is one of the largest annual Christmarkets in the UK! You can find all things festive here; from gifts and trinkets, to locally brewed beers and traditional German hotdogs.
Saturday 8th November - Saturday 21st November at Millenium Square

Year-Round Markets

Kirkgate Market

Kirkgate Market is one of the largest indoor markets in Europe with 800 stalls selling fruit & veg, fish, textiles - not to forget the food court for delicious, freshly made meals! As an added bonus for you M&S lovers, Kirkgate is home to the first ever M&S (1884).
Vicar Lane, Leeds City Centre LS2 7HY

Farmer's Market

At Leed Farmer's Market you'll find locally farmed lamb, pork and beef as well as specialist local cheeses or seasonal fruit and veg. If this isn't enough, there are also craft stalls where you can find one-of-a-kind gifts for your loved ones!
Leeds Farmer's Market takes place on the first and third Sunday of each month
